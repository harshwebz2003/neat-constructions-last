Glass Partition Walls

Highly custom, elegant space division systems designed for modern workspaces and premium homes. Combining structural rigidity, sound reduction, and maximum natural light flow.

Configurations L-Shaped, Straight, or Curved profiles
Glass Thickness 6mm to 12mm heavy tempered safety glass
Glass Types Clear, frosted, semi-frosted, tinted, or stickered
Hardware & Framing Sleek black metal frames or frameless patch fittings
Brochure Note: Available with custom office fittings, sliding/swing doors, and reinforced structures for high-traffic zones like corporate offices, retail lobbies, and co-working environments.

Minimalist Shower Enclosures

Bespoke luxury shower cabins and wet rooms designed to elevate bathroom interiors. Engineered with high-strength anti-corrosive fittings and watertight seals.

Configurations Three-Face, Full-Close, or Doorless wet rooms
Glass Thickness 8mm to 12mm heavy tempered safety glass
Finishes Matte Black, Brushed Gold, Polished Chrome fittings
Glass Treatment Clear, privacy frosted, or luxury grey/bronze tinted
Brochure Note: Utilizes premium grade-304 stainless steel brackets, heavy stabilizing bars, and magnetic closing gaskets to ensure seamless functionality and completely dry floor transitions.

Bespoke Mirror Art & Vanity Panels

Custom-fabricated mirror walls and mosaics crafted using advanced diamond cutting and beveling techniques to amplify light, depth, and sophistication.

Layout Types Vanity mirrors, diamond mosaics, wall art panels
Thickness Range 3mm to 6mm high-fidelity silvered glass
Styling Frameless bevel-edged, or gold-bordered framing
Custom Edge Cuts Straight beveling, diamond-cut grids, polished edges
Brochure Note: Tailored mirror panels are ideal for luxury villa hallways, hotel lobbies, boutique salons, grooming rooms, dance studios, and home gyms.

Smart LED Backlit Mirrors

High-tech vanity mirrors with intelligent touch control sensors and integrated ambient LED strip backlighting, combining modern technology with vanity utility.

Smart Controls Touch Control Sensor, dimming, anti-fog heating
Color Temperatures Warm glow (3000K) to Cool daylight (6000K)
LED Configurations Backlit edge-glow, front-lit frosted strips
Standard Sizes Custom vanity sizing or single fixed large options
Brochure Note: Powered by safety-isolated low voltage transformers, equipped with high-CRI LED arrays to ensure true color reflection for makeup and grooming.

Structural Glass Railings

Heavy-duty tempered laminated glass balustrades designed for staircases, balconies, and terraces. Offers ultimate safety, wind loading resistance, and crystal-clear panoramas.

Configurations Staircase balustrades, Balcony & Terrace rails
Mounting Hardware Frameless bottom-shoes, spigots, or side-bracket clips
Glass Specs 12mm to 19mm heavy tempered laminated glass
Handrail Accents Optional slot-on top handrail (SS316 or Hardwood)
Brochure Note: Compliant with standard QCS safety margins. Engineered to withstand high outdoor wind loads while preserving clean, minimalist modern aesthetics.
